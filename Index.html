ABOUT US At McDonald’s we are moving from awareness to action. Our goal is to have people within our organization working and living to reach their full potential. At McDonald’s, diversity and inclusion are parts of our culture- from the crew room to the Board Room. We are working to achieve this goal every day by creating an environment for everyone to contribute their best. From the start, we’ve been committed to doing the right thing. And we’ve got the policies, programs and practices in place that allow us to make a difference because, what’s good for us, is good for all of us.
